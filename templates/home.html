MAXIMUMIDOL ?

A small project brought to you, with love, by Hello! Ranking.

Eh?

MAXIMUMIDOL takes GIFs created by the Hello! Project community and super-sizes them. All it takes is a single click to get hooked on the thousand GIFs of your favorite idols. It is heavily inspired by MAXGIF which was in turn inspired by ntrnts.com.

But it's better... because idols.

Why?

Because happiness. No really. This was made in the span of a Friday night, mainly to surface the thousand images that we started collecting and because the only way to view them was in a chat room. Unacceptable. There are a lot of plans for this place, but for now, just click. You know you want to.

Oh, all of the source code is available too.
